Branding Concept

Advision combines "Ad" (Advertising) and "Vision", symbolizing a forward-thinking approach to branding. The logo features a stylized open gate in the letter "A", representing new opportunities and accessibility.

Designed with a minimalist art direction, Advision emphasizes bold colors and clean typography, ensuring a strong and memorable visual identity. This branding approach enhances recognition, scalability, and aligns with the brand’s mission—connecting customers with products through impactful marketing.
